Who Am I?

Hi! I'm Jeremy, currently a student at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign, studying Engineering Mechanics with the hopes of a secondary field in fluid mechanics and a minor in Mathematics.

I love all things outdoors, and what better way to combine my appreciation for nature than by using engineering and data to explore the wonders of world.
